Research

My research is focused on developing advanced algorithms and computational methods to solve complex problems in artificial intelligence and data science. I am particularly interested in the following areas:

Deep Learning

Developing novel neural network architectures for processing complex data types with applications in computer vision and natural language processing.

Graph Neural Networks

Exploring graph-based deep learning approaches for analyzing interconnected data structures and relational information in various domains.

Reinforcement Learning

Investigating decision-making algorithms and their applications in autonomous systems and sequential decision problems.

Efficient AI

Designing resource-efficient deep learning algorithms for deployment on edge devices and resource-constrained environments.

Current Research Projects

Interpretable Deep Learning for Medical Imaging

Developing interpretable deep learning models for medical image analysis that provide explanations for their predictions, enhancing trust and adoption in clinical settings.

Ongoing Collaboration with University Hospital

Graph Neural Networks for Drug Discovery

Utilizing graph neural networks to predict molecular properties and drug-target interactions, accelerating the drug discovery process.

Ongoing Collaboration with Pharmaceutical Research Institute

Energy-Efficient Deep Learning

Researching model compression and quantization techniques to reduce the computational and memory requirements of deep neural networks.

Ongoing Funded by Tech Innovation Grant
